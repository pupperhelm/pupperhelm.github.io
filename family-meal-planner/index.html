Family Meal Planner

Weekly planning for a whole household, not one tired cook.

Family Meal Planner helps a household collect recipes, assign lunches and dinners for the week, and build a grocery list from what is actually planned.

What it does

The app is built for real family coordination: shared recipes, role-aware household access, weekly meal slots, and grocery generation tied to the meal plan instead of a disconnected note app.

Core workflow

  • create or join a family space
  • save meals with structured ingredients
  • plan lunches and dinners for the week
  • turn the plan into a grocery list the household can use
