Privacy Policy

Privacy information for Veilcast, a daily oracle and reflection app.

Effective date: March 20, 2026

Veilcast ("we", "our", or "us") provides a mobile entertainment and reflection experience centered on daily oracle-style readings.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how that information is used, and what choices users have.

1. Summary

Veilcast is designed to work without an account.

The app stores most user state locally on the device, including reading history and app preferences. The app also includes Google Mobile Ads for advertising, and that SDK may collect and process certain device and interaction data as described below.

2. Information We Handle

Information stored locally on your device

Veilcast stores app state locally on your device. This may include:

  • an installation identifier
  • reading history
  • reading feedback you choose to save
  • progression and achievement state
  • app preferences such as sound settings
  • basic in-app counters such as reveal and share events

This locally stored information is used to provide app functionality such as repeatable daily readings, saved history, progression, and personalization.

Advertising and analytics-related data handled by third-party SDKs

Veilcast uses Google Mobile Ads to display ads. According to Google's documentation for the Google Mobile Ads SDK, that SDK may automatically collect and share certain data, including:

  • IP address
  • app interaction information
  • diagnostic information
  • device and account identifiers, including advertising-related identifiers where applicable

This data may be used for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention.

For more information, see:
Google Mobile Ads SDK Play data disclosure
Google Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Information

We use locally stored app information to:

  • generate and display readings
  • maintain your reading vault and progression state
  • remember your preferences
  • support app behavior such as streaks, unlocks, and saved feedback

Advertising-related data handled by third-party SDKs may be used to:

  • serve ads
  • measure ad performance
  • detect fraud and abuse
  • improve SDK reliability and diagnostics

4. Data Sharing

We do not operate a user account system or a backend service for Veilcast user profiles.

However, third-party service providers used in the app, including Google Mobile Ads, may collect and process data as described in their own documentation and policies.

5. Data Retention

Locally stored app data remains on your device until you remove it by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's local storage.

Third-party data retention is governed by the policies of the applicable provider.

6. Your Choices

You may stop use of the app at any time.

You may remove locally stored Veilcast data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage from your Android device settings.

Advertising-related identifiers may also be controlled through Android privacy and advertising settings, subject to platform availability.

7. Children

Veilcast is not directed to children.

8. Security

We do not promise that any system is perfectly secure. Where third-party SDKs transmit data, those providers may apply their own transport and security protections according to their documentation.

9. International Users

If you use Veilcast outside your home country, third-party service providers may process data in other jurisdictions according to their own privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
