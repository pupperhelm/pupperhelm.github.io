Instant Voice Notes

Open the app. Speak. Save the note.

Instant Voice Notes is an Android voice-to-text notes app built for quick capture. It opens straight into recording, saves dictated notes locally, and keeps same-hour thoughts together with timestamps.

What it does

The app is designed for moments when typing is too slow. Start capture from the app, a home-screen widget, or a supported phone shortcut, then speak naturally and save the transcript as a local note.

Core workflow

  • open directly into voice capture
  • save dictated transcripts on the device
  • merge captures from the same hour with timestamps
  • search, sort, filter, edit, join, and delete notes
  • send saved notes to note apps or email through Android sharing
