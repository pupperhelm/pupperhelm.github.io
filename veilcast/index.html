What it is

Instead of a generic horoscope feed, Veilcast frames each session as a focused ritual. A runic twenty-sided die, theme-led readings, and a persistent vault make it feel more like an artifact than a content stream. The current public page reflects the beta version of the product.

Inside the app

  • daily readings shaped by theme, seal, and symbol
  • a saved Vault for prior casts and reflections
  • discoverable seal and codex-style progression
  • feedback and resonance signals stored on-device
