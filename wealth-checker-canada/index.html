About the app

Wealth Checker Canada is a simple Canadian finance education app that compares household income and net worth against public benchmark estimates.

Enter your age, region, household type, annual income, and net worth to see percentile-style context, distribution charts, and age-adjusted comparison curves.

The app uses public Canadian data-source references and presents results as estimates for education and planning context. It is not financial, tax, legal, or investment advice.

No account is required, and the calculator runs locally on your device.
