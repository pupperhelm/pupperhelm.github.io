Wealth Checker Canada

Privacy Notice

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Wealth Checker Canada is an educational calculator for comparing household income and net worth against public Canadian benchmark estimates.

Data entered in the app

The app asks for age, region, household type, annual income, and net worth so it can calculate an on-device comparison. These values are processed locally inside the app.

Data collection

The Android app does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal information. It does not require account creation.

External links

The app includes links to public data sources. Opening those links may launch the user's browser, and the destination website may have its own privacy practices.

Financial disclaimer

Wealth Checker Canada is for education and planning context only. It is not financial, tax, legal, or investment advice.
